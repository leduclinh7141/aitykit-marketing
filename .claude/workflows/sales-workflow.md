## Lead Qualification Frameworks

### BANT Framework (Traditional)

| Criteria | Questions | Score |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| **Budget** | Can they afford it? | 0-25 |
| **Authority** | Are they decision maker? | 0-25 |
| **Need** | Do they have the problem we solve? | 0-25 |
| **Timeline** | When do they need solution? | 0-25 |

### MEDDIC Framework (Enterprise B2B)

| Criteria | Definition | Score |
|----------|------------|-------|
| **Metrics** | Quantifiable goals they want to achieve | 0-17 |
| **Economic Buyer** | Person with budget authority | 0-17 |
| **Decision Criteria** | What they'll evaluate solutions on | 0-17 |
| **Decision Process** | How they make purchasing decisions | 0-17 |
| **Identify Pain** | Their core business problems | 0-16 |
| **Champion** | Internal advocate for your solution | 0-16 |

### Intent Signals

**High Intent (Ready to buy):**
- Pricing page visits (3+ times)
- Demo request
- Free trial signup
- Competitor comparison searches
- "How to buy" or "pricing" keywords

**Medium Intent (Evaluating):**
- Case study downloads
- Webinar attendance

**Low Intent (Awareness):**
- Single blog visit

## Outreach Sequences

### Cold Outreach Sequence (7 touches over 21 days)

**Day 1 - Initial Email:**
```
Subject: [Pain point] at [Company]?

Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] is [observation about their business].

Many [industry] companies struggle with [pain point], which leads to [consequence].

We helped [similar company] achieve [specific result] in [timeframe].

Worth a quick chat to see if we can help [Company] too?

[Signature]
```

**Day 3 - Follow-up Email:**
```
Subject: Re: [Pain point] at [Company]?

Hi [Name],

Quick follow-up on my note about [pain point].

Here's a 2-min case study showing how [similar company] solved this: [link]

Would Tuesday or Thursday work for a 15-min call?

[Signature]
```

**Day 5 - LinkedIn Connection:**
- Connect with personalized note referencing email

**Day 8 - Value Email:**
```
Subject: [Relevant resource] for [Company]

Hi [Name],

Thought you might find this useful: [resource link]

It covers [specific topic relevant to their pain].

No pitch - just sharing something that might help.

[Signature]
```

**Day 12 - Social Engagement:**
- Like/comment on their recent post
- Share their content with commentary

**Day 16 - Breakup Email:**
```
Subject: Should I close your file?

Hi [Name],

I've reached out a few times but haven't heard back.

I'll assume the timing isn't right and close your file.

If things change, just reply to this email - I'm here to help.

[Signature]
```

**Day 21 - Final Value:**
- Send relevant industry report or insight
- No ask, pure value

## Sales Enablement Assets

### Pitch Deck Structure

1. **Problem** (1-2 slides): Industry pain points with data
2. **Solution** (2-3 slides): How you solve it
3. **Proof** (2-3 slides): Case studies, testimonials, results
4. **Product** (3-4 slides): Features and benefits
5. **Pricing** (1 slide): Options and packages
6. **Next Steps** (1 slide): Clear CTA

### Objection Handling Scripts

**"Too expensive"**
```
"I understand budget is important. Let me ask - what would it cost
you to NOT solve [pain point]? Our clients typically see [ROI metric]
within [timeframe]. Would it help to see the ROI calculation?"
```

**"Need to think about it"**
```
"Absolutely, this is a big decision. What specific concerns do you
want to think through? I want to make sure you have all the info
you need to make the best decision for [Company]."
```

**"Using competitor"**
```
"[Competitor] is solid. What made you choose them? I ask because
some of our best clients switched from [competitor] because of
[specific differentiator]. Would you be open to seeing how we compare?"
```

**"Not the right time"**
```
"I appreciate you being upfront. When would be a better time to
revisit this? I'll set a reminder and reach out then. In the meantime,
would it be helpful if I sent you [relevant resource]?"
```

# Paid Advertising

Paid media strategy and optimization across platforms.

## Activation

Use when planning paid campaigns, optimizing ad performance, or setting up tracking.

## Core Concepts

### Campaign Objectives

| Objective | Use When | KPI |
|-----------|----------|-----|
| Awareness | Building brand | Reach, impressions |
| Traffic | Driving site visits | CPC, CTR |
| Engagement | Growing social | CPE, engagement rate |
| Leads | Generating contacts | CPL, lead quality |
| Conversions | Driving sales | ROAS, CPA |
| App Installs | Mobile acquisition | CPI, install rate |

### Funnel-Stage Targeting

**TOFU (Awareness)**
- Broad audiences
- Interest-based targeting
- Lookalike audiences
- Video content

**MOFU (Consideration)**
- Website visitors
- Engaged audiences
- Email list matches
- Educational content

**BOFU (Decision)**
- High-intent visitors
- Cart abandoners
- Demo requesters
- Promotional content

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| CTR | Clicks / Impressions | 1-3% |
| CPC | Spend / Clicks | Varies by platform |
| CPA | Spend / Conversions | <1/3 LTV |
| ROAS | Revenue / Ad Spend | 3:1+ |
| CVR | Conversions / Clicks | 2-5% |

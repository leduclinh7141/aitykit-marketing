# Social Media

Social media strategy, content creation, and platform optimization.

## Activation

Use when creating social content, developing engagement strategies, or optimizing for platform algorithms.

## Core Concepts

### Platform Selection

| Platform | Best For | Audience | Content Type |
|----------|----------|----------|--------------|
| LinkedIn | B2B, professional | Decision makers | Long-form, documents |
| Twitter/X | News, tech, B2B | Early adopters | Short-form, threads |
| Instagram | B2C, visual brands | Millennials/Gen Z | Reels, carousels |
| TikTok | B2C, entertainment | Gen Z, younger | Short video |
| Facebook | Local, community | Broad, older | Video, groups |
| YouTube | Education, how-to | All ages | Long-form video |

### Content Strategy

**Content Pillars**
- Educational (teach something)
- Entertaining (make them laugh/feel)
- Inspirational (motivate them)
- Promotional (sell something)

**Content Mix**
- 40% Educational
- 30% Entertaining/Engaging
- 20% Community/UGC
- 10% Promotional

### Engagement Tactics

- Respond to comments quickly
- Ask questions in posts
- Use polls and interactive features
- Tag relevant accounts
- Join conversations in your niche

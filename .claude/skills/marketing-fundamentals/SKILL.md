# Marketing Fundamentals

Core marketing concepts, psychology, and frameworks for effective marketing.

## Activation

Use when working on any marketing task requiring foundational knowledge, understanding customer psychology, or applying marketing frameworks.

## Core Concepts

### Marketing Funnel (TOFU/MOFU/BOFU)

**Top of Funnel (TOFU) - Awareness**
- Problem-aware to solution-aware
- Content: Educational, thought leadership
- Metrics: Reach, impressions, traffic

**Middle of Funnel (MOFU) - Consideration**
- Solution-aware to product-aware
- Content: Comparison, case studies
- Metrics: Engagement, leads, MQLs

**Bottom of Funnel (BOFU) - Decision**
- Product-aware to most-aware
- Content: Demos, trials, testimonials
- Metrics: SQLs, opportunities, conversion

**Post-Purchase - Retention**
- Onboarding, retention, advocacy
- Content: How-to, community, referral
- Metrics: NPS, retention, expansion

### Customer Journey Mapping

1. **Awareness**: How do they discover you?
2. **Research**: What information do they seek?
3. **Consideration**: What options do they compare?
4. **Decision**: What triggers the purchase?
5. **Experience**: What happens post-purchase?
6. **Advocacy**: What makes them recommend you?

### Value Proposition Design

**Jobs-to-be-Done Framework**
- Functional jobs (tasks to complete)
- Social jobs (how they want to be perceived)
- Emotional jobs (how they want to feel)

**Value Proposition Canvas**
- Gain creators (how you create gains)
- Pain relievers (how you eliminate pains)
- Products/services (what you offer)

### Positioning

**Elements of Positioning**
- Target audience
- Frame of reference
- Point of differentiation
- Reason to believe

**Positioning Statement Template**
> For [target audience] who [need/opportunity], [product name] is a [category] that [key benefit]. Unlike [competitors], we [unique differentiator].

# Content Strategy

Content planning, creation, and distribution strategy.

## Activation

Use when planning content calendars, developing content pillars, or creating distribution strategies.

## Core Concepts

### Content Pillars

Content pillars are the main themes your content revolves around.

**Criteria for Good Pillars:**
- Aligned with business goals
- Relevant to audience pain points
- Differentiates from competitors
- Sustainable long-term
- SEO opportunity

**Example Structure:**
- Pillar 1: Industry insights
- Pillar 2: How-to/tutorials
- Pillar 3: Customer success
- Pillar 4: Product updates
- Pillar 5: Company culture

### Content Types by Funnel Stage

| Stage | Content Type | Goal |
|-------|--------------|------|
| TOFU | Blog, social, video | Attract |
| MOFU | Ebooks, webinars, guides | Educate |
| BOFU | Case studies, demos, comparison | Convert |
| Retention | Tutorials, community | Retain |

### Content Mix

**70-20-10 Rule:**
- 70% proven content types
- 20% iterative improvements
- 10% experimental formats

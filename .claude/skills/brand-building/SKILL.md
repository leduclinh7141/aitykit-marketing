# Brand Building

Brand strategy, identity, and positioning.

## Activation

Use when developing brand strategy, creating brand guidelines, or working on positioning.

## Core Concepts

### Brand Elements

| Element | Definition |
|---------|------------|
| Brand Purpose | Why you exist beyond profit |
| Brand Vision | Where you're going |
| Brand Mission | How you'll get there |
| Brand Values | What you stand for |
| Brand Personality | Human traits that describe you |
| Brand Voice | How you communicate |
| Brand Promise | What customers can expect |

### Brand Architecture

**Branded House**
- One master brand
- Sub-brands under umbrella
- Example: Google (Maps, Drive, etc.)

**House of Brands**
- Independent brands
- Parent company behind scenes
- Example: P&G (Tide, Pampers, etc.)

**Hybrid**
- Mix of both approaches
- Strategic decisions per product

### Brand Equity

**Components:**
- Brand awareness
- Brand associations
- Perceived quality
- Brand loyalty

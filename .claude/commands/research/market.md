# Market Research

## Objective
Conduct comprehensive market research for the specified market or industry.

## Workflow

1. **Scope Definition**
   - Market boundaries
   - Research questions
   - Data requirements
   - Timeline

2. **Data Gathering**
   - Industry reports
   - Market statistics
   - Competitor landscape
   - Customer insights

3. **Trend Analysis**
   - Growth trends
   - Technology shifts
   - Consumer behavior changes
   - Regulatory changes

4. **Competitive Assessment**
   - Key players
   - Market share
   - Positioning
   - Strengths/weaknesses

5. **Opportunity Identification**
   - Market gaps
   - Underserved segments
   - Emerging needs
   - Entry barriers

## Research Framework

```markdown
## Market Overview
- Size: $[X]B
- Growth: [X]% CAGR
- Key Drivers: [List]

## Competitive Landscape
| Player | Share | Positioning |
|--------|-------|-------------|

## Trends
1. [Trend 1] - Impact
2. [Trend 2] - Impact

## Opportunities
1. [Gap/Opportunity]
2. [Gap/Opportunity]

## Recommendations
1. [Action]
2. [Action]
```

## Output
- Market research report
- Competitive matrix
- Opportunity assessment

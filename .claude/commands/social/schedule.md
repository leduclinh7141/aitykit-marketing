# Social Media Schedule

## Objective
Create an optimized social media posting schedule for the specified platforms and timeframe.

## Workflow

1. **Optimal Timing Analysis**
   - Best posting times by platform
   - Audience activity patterns
   - Time zone considerations
   - Day-of-week performance

2. **Content Mapping**
   - Content types per platform
   - Post format mix (text, image, video)
   - Content pillars allocation
   - Promotional vs value ratio

3. **Calendar Creation**
   - Daily posting schedule
   - Weekly themes
   - Monthly campaigns
   - Special dates/events

4. **Frequency Setting**
   - Posts per day per platform
   - Optimal spacing
   - Engagement windows
   - Story/ephemeral content

5. **Campaign Alignment**
   - Integrate campaign content
   - Coordinate with launches
   - Cross-platform synchronization

## Platform Guidelines

| Platform | Posts/Day | Best Times | Content Focus |
|----------|-----------|------------|---------------|
| LinkedIn | 1-2 | 8-10am, 12pm | Professional, thought leadership |
| Twitter/X | 3-5 | 9am, 12pm, 5pm | News, engagement, trending |
| Instagram | 1-2 | 11am, 2pm, 7pm | Visual, lifestyle, stories |
| Facebook | 1-2 | 1-4pm | Community, video, events |
| TikTok | 1-3 | 7-9pm | Entertainment, trends |

## Output
- Complete posting calendar
- Content slot allocations
- Platform-specific guidelines

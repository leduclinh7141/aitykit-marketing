# Lead Qualification Framework

## Objective
Create comprehensive lead qualification criteria for your product or service.

## Workflow

1. **Define ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)**
   - Company characteristics
   - Industry and vertical
   - Company size/revenue
   - Technology stack
   - Budget indicators

2. **Create Scoring Dimensions**
   - Demographic factors
   - Firmographic factors
   - Behavioral signals
   - Engagement metrics
   - Intent indicators

3. **Set Qualification Thresholds**
   - MQL (Marketing Qualified Lead) criteria
   - SQL (Sales Qualified Lead) criteria
   - Point thresholds per stage
   - Score decay rules

4. **Define Disqualification Criteria**
   - Hard disqualifiers
   - Soft disqualifiers
   - Requalification paths

5. **Design Handoff Protocols**
   - Marketing to Sales handoff
   - Required information
   - SLA definitions
   - Feedback loops

## Output
- Lead qualification playbook
- Scoring rubric
- Handoff documentation

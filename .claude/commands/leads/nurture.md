# Lead Nurture Sequence Design

## Objective
Design an effective email nurture sequence for the specified segment or funnel stage.

## Workflow

1. **Journey Stage Mapping**
   - Identify current awareness level
   - Define desired next step
   - Map content needs
   - Understand objections

2. **Content Strategy**
   - Educational content needs
   - Pain point addressing
   - Solution positioning
   - Social proof integration

3. **Email Sequence Design**
   - Number of emails
   - Timing between sends
   - Subject line strategy
   - CTA progression

4. **Trigger Configuration**
   - Entry triggers
   - Exit conditions
   - Branch logic
   - Re-entry rules

5. **Content Brief Creation**
   - Email 1: [Purpose]
   - Email 2: [Purpose]
   - ...and so on

## Sequence Types

| Type | Length | Goal |
|------|--------|------|
| Welcome | 5-7 emails | Educate & engage |
| Nurture | 8-12 emails | Build trust |
| Trial | 7-10 emails | Convert to paid |
| Re-engagement | 3-5 emails | Win back |

## Output
- Complete nurture sequence map
- Email content briefs
- Automation workflow diagram

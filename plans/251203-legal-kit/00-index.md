## Executive Summary

**ClaudeKit Legal** is an AI-powered legal automation kit designed for law firms, in-house legal teams, and legal professionals. It provides pre-configured AI agents for contract review, legal research, e-discovery, compliance monitoring, and practice management.

### ClaudeKit Legal Differentiators

- **Pre-configured agent teams** vs custom setup
- **Multi-model flexibility** vs single vendor lock-in
- **Affordable pricing** ($50-150/user/mo target)
- **MCP integrations** for document management
- **Practice management** built-in

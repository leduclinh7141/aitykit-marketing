## 1.3 Sales Enablement AI Capabilities

### Content & Battlecards

- **Automatic surfacing**: AI pushes relevant content during calls
- **Competitor detection**: Mentions trigger battlecard display
- **Personalized proposals**: Auto-generate based on buyer industry
- **Real-time coaching**: In-call suggestions and prompts

### Revenue Intelligence

- **Conversation analysis**: Record, transcribe, analyze all calls
- **Deal health scoring**: AI predicts deal outcomes
- **Pipeline inspection**: Identify at-risk deals early
- **Forecast accuracy**: Data-driven revenue predictions

### CRM Automation

- **Auto-logging**: Calls, emails, meetings logged automatically
- **Data enrichment**: Contact/account data kept current
- **Task prioritization**: AI suggests next best actions
- **Workflow triggers**: Automated follow-up sequences

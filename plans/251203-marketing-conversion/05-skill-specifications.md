## 5.2 Skill: marketing-fundamentals

**Purpose:** Core marketing concepts, psychology, and frameworks

### SKILL.md Content Outline

```markdown
# Marketing Fundamentals

## Activation
Use when working on any marketing task requiring foundational knowledge.

## Core Concepts

### Marketing Funnel (TOFU/MOFU/BOFU)
- Awareness → Interest → Consideration → Intent → Purchase → Loyalty
- Content types appropriate for each stage
- Metrics for each stage

### Customer Journey Mapping
- Touchpoint identification
- Pain point mapping
- Opportunity identification

### Value Proposition Design
- Jobs-to-be-done framework
- Gain creators and pain relievers
- Unique selling propositions

### Positioning
- Competitive positioning
- Perceptual mapping
- Differentiation strategies
```

### Reference Files

**funnel-psychology.md**
- TOFU: Problem-aware to solution-aware
- MOFU: Solution-aware to product-aware
- BOFU: Product-aware to most-aware
- Post-purchase: Retention and advocacy

**conversion-principles.md**
- Cialdini's 6 principles of persuasion
- Loss aversion and urgency
- Social proof mechanics
- Trust signals

**copywriting-frameworks.md**
- AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)
- PAS (Problem, Agitate, Solution)
- BAB (Before, After, Bridge)
- 4 Ps (Promise, Picture, Proof, Push)
- FOMO Formula

**marketing-metrics.md**
- Acquisition: CAC, CPA, CPL
- Engagement: CTR, open rate, bounce rate
- Conversion: CVR, AOV, RPV
- Retention: LTV, churn rate, NPS
- ROI: ROAS, MER, payback period
